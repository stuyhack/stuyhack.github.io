whiteHatAcademy

whiteHatAcademy is the Stuyvesant High School Cyber Security Club, founded by Ethan Cheng, Loren Maggiore, and Yicheng Wang. Together, we will be teaching topics such as:

  • Cryptography
  • Digital Forensics and Steganography
  • Binary Exploitation
  • Network Vulnerability Exploitation
  • Reverse Engineering

That to ensure functional code, loopholes will be instituted by their programmers, giving their power to the exploiter.   • No program is without bugs.
  • No code can be without vulnerability.
  • No information connected to a network can be 100% protected.

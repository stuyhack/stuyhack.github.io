StuyHack

StuyHack, formerly known as whiteHatAcademy, is Stuyvesant High School's cybersecurity club, founded by Ethan Cheng, Yicheng Wang, and Loren Maggiore in 2015.

Topics

Cryptography

Cryptography is what hides your data using (usually) mathematical algorithms. It's what prevents people from reading "23b3e8d214366ce9019b53eabae84 bc3f14ba1b2a67 aa07a1b7070c739b5fe7c" as "Hey Scott, my password is abc123."

Web Exploitation

Web exploitation is finding vulnerabilities in web applications and websites that allow unauthorized or unwanted access the webserver and its data.

Binary Exploitation

Binary exploitation is finding vulnerabilities in compiled executables. In binary exploitation, you explore and play around with memory to find hidden data and access functions.

Digital Forensics

Digital forensics is a broad field of analyzing logs, crawling through data, and analyzing files to find information. The field ranges from sifting through internet packets to find who DDOSed you to recovering deleted files to finding hidden files.

Reverse Engineering

Reverse engineering is where you try and find a way to undo an algorithm, usually a cipher, to undo some sort of encryption to find the hidden data.
